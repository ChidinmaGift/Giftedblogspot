Why we can't help contradicting administration on Biafra — IPOB

ON JULY 13, 20209:09


The Indigenous People of Biafra, IPOB yesterday couldn't help contradicting an announcement attributed to the Special Adviser to President Muhammadu Buhari on Media and Publicity, Femi Adesina that President Muhammadu Buhari was not against the individuals of Biafra.

IPOB said the announcement is a finished misrepresentation and can just mislead the naïve however unquestionably not those acquainted with history.

As per an announcement by Comrade Emma Powerful, Media and Publicity Secretary of IPOB, "reality rather, is that individuals of Biafra have kept on being casualties of state mistreatment and determined disposal. Biafrans have been butchered at the smallest incitement in a few events since the unholy amalgamation of Nigeria in 1914. Nigeria Government and her shrewd accomplices are supporting the killings and cutthroat slaughter of Biafrans since 1945 till date.

"Fear monger gatherings and Boko Harams have kept on butchering with total surrender, Christians in Nigeria especially Southern Kaduna Indigenes and Biafrans in their ranches and towns, yet the Federal Government has not done a lot to stop the annihilation! Executioner herders have become a troublesome assignment for the central government which unexpectedly labeled unarmed IPOB a dread gathering with the speed of light. How does an administration which spoils all inclusive recognized fear gatherings yet stifles and slaughters tranquil fomenters need to demonstrate its blamelessness of gore?"
