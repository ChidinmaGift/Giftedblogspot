Davido buys daughter Imade a gift as
she graduates from kindergarten

Category: Latest Photo News, Gist, Nigeria Entertainment News

- Imade Adeleke recently graduated from kindergarten and is set for nursery school.

- The beautiful girl who is the first child of Davido got a gift from her superstar dad Davido, the little girl also shared adorable photos of the gifts gotten for her by her superstar Dad Davido.

- Imade shared photos of herself posing with a cake, her certificate and in another, on a bike gifted to her by her dad for her graduation.

Nigerian singer, David Adeleke, better known as Davido, recently had cause to celebrate as his beautiful daughter and first child with Sophie Momodu, Imade, recently graduated from kindergarten to a Nursery School.

The beautiful 5-year-old took to her personal Instagram page to share some adorable photos of herself as a kindergarten graduate, set for nursery school, and she seemed so happy and excited.

In the photos, Imade is seen posing with a cake that has"congrats Imade on your graduation." In another photo, she is seen showing off her certificate and then in another, posing on her cute and adorable bicycle, gotten from her superstar Dad, Davido.
